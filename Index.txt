        <div class="q1">
          <details>
            <summary>How much does an average chip repair cost?</summary>

              Most small rock chips are smaller than a nickle coin and cost
              between $65-$75 to repair.
            </p>
          </details>
        </div>
        <div class="q2">
          <details>
            <summary>
              How long does it take to repair a windshield chip?
            </summary>
            <p>
              Most rock chips take less than 30 minutes to repair. That
              includes prepping the area, injecting and curing resin.
            </p>
          </details>
        </div>
        <div class="q3">

            </p>

              wash).

              How can I know if my windshield chip will crack more?
